FAQ

Common
Questions.

Straight answers about decks, kitchens, permits, pricing and timelines. No jargon.

A timber deck typically costs $600–$1,200 per m² depending on timber species, size and access. A standard 20m² hardwood deck usually runs $15,000–$20,000 installed. Composite decking costs more upfront but is lower maintenance. We provide free, itemised quotes.
Most decks under 800mm in height within standard setbacks don't need a building permit. Larger decks, roofed structures and decks in bushfire or heritage overlay zones typically do. We advise on permit requirements before any work starts.
Merbau is the most popular — durable, affordable and handles Melbourne's temperature swings well. Spotted Gum and Blackbutt are premium options. For low-maintenance, composite decking (Trex, ModWood) is increasingly popular. We work with all of these.
A standard kitchen reno with new cabinetry, benchtops and joinery takes 2–4 weeks. Custom cabinetry and structural changes can extend this. We handle all carpentry and joinery, and coordinate with other trades where needed.
