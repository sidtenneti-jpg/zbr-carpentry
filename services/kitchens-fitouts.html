Common Questions

How long does a kitchen renovation take?

A standard kitchen reno — new cabinetry, benchtops, joinery — takes 2–4 weeks. Custom or structural changes can extend this. We give a realistic timeline before starting.

Can a carpenter do a full kitchen renovation?

Yes — carpentry is the core trade in a kitchen renovation. ZBR handles all cabinetry, joinery and fitting work. For plumbing, electrical and tiling, we can recommend trusted trades or coordinate directly.

How much does a kitchen renovation cost in Melbourne?

A mid-range kitchen renovation (new cabinetry, benchtops, joinery, no structural changes) typically runs $15,000–$35,000 for the carpentry and materials. Custom or high-spec kitchens can exceed this. We provide free itemised quotes.
