ZBR Carpentry provides the full range of general carpentry services across Melbourne. From structural framing and timber repairs to custom builds, pergolas and fencing — if it's timber, ZBR handles it. Owner-operated, so you deal with the tradesman doing the job, not a project manager.

New framing for extensions, alterations and additions. Structural repairs, wall removal (in consultation with your builder/engineer) and sub-floor work.

Common Questions

What general carpentry work does ZBR do?

ZBR handles most residential carpentry — framing, pergolas, fencing, repairs, built-in wardrobes, custom timber work and more. If it's a timber or joinery job, call and we'll let you know if it's in scope.

Do you do small jobs?

Yes — ZBR takes on jobs of all sizes. A fence repair, a wardrobe fit-out, a single door frame. Small jobs are quoted honestly and priced fairly.

How quickly can you start?

Availability varies, but ZBR typically books within 1–3 weeks for most jobs. Call 0431 213 585 or submit a quote request and we'll give you an honest timeline.
