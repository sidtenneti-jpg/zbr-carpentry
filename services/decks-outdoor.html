Common Questions

How much does a deck cost in Melbourne?

Hardwood decking runs approximately $600–$1,200 per m² installed. A standard 20m² deck is typically $15,000–$20,000. Composite decking is slightly more. We provide free, itemised quotes with no obligation.

What timber is best for Melbourne?

Merbau is the most popular and cost-effective. It handles Melbourne's heat, cold and UV well. Spotted Gum and Blackbutt are premium alternatives with excellent durability. Composite is the best low-maintenance option.

Do I need a permit for a deck in Melbourne?

Most decks under 800mm above natural ground level within standard setbacks don't require a building permit in Victoria. Larger decks, roofed structures and properties in heritage or bushfire overlays may require one. We advise before starting any work.
